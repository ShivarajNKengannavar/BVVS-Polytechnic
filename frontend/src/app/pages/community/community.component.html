Community College & Outreach

Sanctioned by MHRD-AICTE since 2013 — providing skill-integrated, affordable and lifelong learning for underprivileged communities across North Karnataka.

Community College

Our B.V.V.S. Polytechnic was sanctioned with a Community College under MHRD-AICTE, New Delhi on 12th July 2013 for the year 2013-2014. As per MHRD guidelines, the Community College started from 10th October 2013. The total amount sanctioned for 2013–2016 is Rs. 3,38,85,000 for recurring and non-recurring expenditures.

Objectives of Community College

  • Provide opportunities for skill-integrated, flexible, relevant, affordable and lifelong learning for all people in communities to acquire employable skills for their livelihood.
  • Provide basic skills, knowledge and attitude for self/wage employment to students in their own villages or nearby areas.
  • Offer skill up-gradation programs or adoption of appropriate technologies for enhancing employment prospects.

Why a Community College Here?

  • The area is rural-based with people dependent on the agriculture sector for livelihood.
  • Lack of employable skills for people in and around the area.
  • Nearly 200 villages submerged in the Upper Krishna Project — rehabilitation of manpower for new jobs is challenging and inevitable.
  • Number of Sugar, Cement and Iron & Steel industries established nearby requiring skilled workforce.
  • Focus on producing entrepreneurs to create local employment opportunities.
