Our Legacy

Shaping Technical
Minds Since 1958

B.V.V.S. Polytechnic Bagalkot was established under the Basaveshwar Veerashaiva Vidyavardhak Sangha — a 126-institution network. Our institute received academic autonomy in 1999, is the only polytechnic in Karnataka to run a Community College sanctioned by MHRD-AICTE, and remains NBA Accredited through 2026.

🎯

Vision

To be an exemplary polytechnic serving society by inculcating technical and non-technical expertise for better career choices with human values.

🚀

Mission

Quality education in technical and professional fields, with emphasis on industry-institute interaction and community upliftment.
