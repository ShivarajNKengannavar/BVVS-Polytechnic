About the Library

The BVVS Polytechnic Library is a state-of-the-art digital library designed to meet the knowledge needs of students and faculty across all 9 diploma programmes. With nearly 32,000 volumes and over 9,000 titles covering all engineering disciplines, it is one of the best-stocked polytechnic libraries in North Karnataka.

The library subscribes to 35 National and International Journals covering Electrical, Mechanical, Civil, Computer Science, Electronics and other engineering fields. The DELNET (Developing Library Network) facility provides access to a vast network of libraries across India, greatly expanding the resources available to students.
